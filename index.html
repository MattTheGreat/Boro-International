2016 'Boro International: A Multicultural Celebration

Saturday, September 10th

'Boro International is a celebration of the rich cultural diversity in Murfreesboro and Middle Tennessee.
By celebrating diversity, a genuine sense of international/multicultural unity and friendship can be fostered.
