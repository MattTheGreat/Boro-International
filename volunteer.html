Rules and Regulations

‘Boro International and Cannonsburgh Village are not responsible for damage or theft during the Celebration. ‘Boro International reserves the right to accept or reject any item sold or displayed during this event. All vendors are responsible for their own tents, tables and chairs. The booth size is 10’x10’. ‘Boro International runs from 10:00 a.m. until 3:00 p.m.. Set-up may begin at 800 a.m. and vendors MAY NOT DISMANTLE their booths until 3:00 p.m.. Vehicles will not be allowed in the Village between the hours of 9:30 a.m. and 3:00 p.m. All vendors and exhibitors are responsible for the clean-up of their booth space and surrounding area throughout the day and especially upon vacating the premises after the Celebration is over. All grease must be disposed of off the premises of Cannonsburgh Village. ‘Boro International will supply advertisement for this event. ‘Boro International reserves the right to use your photos for publicity purposes and to give your name to publications if they wish additional information on you or your items for sale. If you do not wish your name to be disclosed, please note this request on your application. ‘Boro International shall have the authority to cancel all activities during the Celebration due to severe weather or an act of God. All vendors and exhibitors will be notified upon approval for placement purposes inside or outside the village. If you have questions concerning the type of craft, food or entertainment you can provide, please contact Tena Bailey at Tena.Bailey@cityschools.net .
